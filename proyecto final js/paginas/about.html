About us

I'm an expat living in Switzerland. My family is still back in Argentina, almost 14.000kms away. During my first year here they tried to send me a birthday gift, something they could order from there and would arrive directly to my door by post. But between the language differences and the ignorance of how this foreign country works, they never made it. And that's how 'Breakfast at Switzerland's' came to life: an user-friendly, expat-proof company that takes care of the production, preparation and delivery of an universal favorite: a nice, warm and delicious breakfast.
